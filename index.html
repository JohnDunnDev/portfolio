ABOUT ME

I'm a 30 year old self-taught full-stack developer from South Yorkshire.

I've wondered how software was made for as long as I can remember, whenever I used a computer or played a video game as a kid I always wondered what was going on under the hood but I thought software development was for geniuses and I felt I didn't fit the bill.

In school we had one lesson where we were taught about HTML, we created a "Hello world" page with some inline styling, and I played around with styling MySpace in my rebellious teenage years. Fast forward a decade and I got fed up of working jobs I wasn't invested in so I started learning something I've always been fascinated by. Nowadays, I like building cool things and learning how to build cooler things.

When I'm not designing or writing code for my next project I like to play video games, play guitar, and spend time with my fiance, kids and dogs.
